Humour

Humour can help you cope with the unbearable so that you can stay on the bright side of things until the bright side actually comes along.

Whether it's giggling at something someone said or laughing out loud at a comedy show, a good laugh is healthy for you.

Stand-ups and funny people are performing a revolutionary service for public health. And it happens to be hilarious
