Yoga & Meditation

Stressed? Here's how you can do yoga for mental peace and strees management

Yoga, has additional benefits, it affets the moody by elevating levels of a brain chemical called GABA, which help with better mood and decrease anxiety.
Here are a few asans you can try in order to achieve inner peace and rid your mind of any lingering stress.

Meditation

Meditation can give you a sense of calm, peace and balance that can benefit both your emotional well-being and your overall health. And these benefits don't end when your meditation session ends . It can help carry you more calmly through your day and may help you manage symptoms of certain medical conditions.

Yoga asanas

Anjaneyasana

This pose is also known as “the high lunge”. Doing the crescent variation of this exercise is known to help improve your blood flow, calm your mind.

Chakrasana

Known as the circle pose, this is a bit of a tricky asana but it helps your calmness and focus.

Vrikasana

Commonly known as “the tree pose”, this asana is very basic and helps improve your sense of self and centres your body and mind.

Vajrasana

This is a meditative pose that is known to improve focus, calmness and digestion.

Natarajasana

Commonly referred to as “the dancer’s pose”, this asana is great for mood balancing. It can be beneficial for those who are fighting depression and anxiety.
